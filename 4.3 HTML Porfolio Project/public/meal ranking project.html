The Favorite Meal According to Amalia

My top 3 meals of all-time

Sunny Side Up Egg

This is my first favorite meal. It is easy to store and prepare, cheap, high in macro and micro nutrient.

Avocado

This is my second favorite meal. Even the price is quite expensive but its taste is so yummy and do not need to take extra effort to cook it

Chicken

I really like to eat fried chicken crispy. It is crunchy, salty, and umami. It is my option when I am bored to eat sunny side up egg or avocado.
